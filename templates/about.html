About Hemmingwurst

Hemmingwurst is a light riff on Hemmingway App, an app which "makes your writing clear and bold." I don't mean to diminish the work that they've done - I actually think what they're doing is pretty interesting - I just couldn't resist the pun. Plus, this was a great chance to try out Flask which was incredibly easy to get set up and working with Heroku.

Heroku has some very handy instructions for setting up Flask apps, which are worth checking out. It only took me a couple hours to go from concept to completion, which goes to show how easy Flask and Heroku have made it to get started.
